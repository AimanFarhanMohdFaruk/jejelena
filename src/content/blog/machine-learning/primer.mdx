## Introduction

In this series, I'll be sharing my journey into the world of machine learning. I'll be sharing my experiences, challenges, and learnings as I embark on this journey.

## The objective of this series

For joy. I have yet to record my journey publicly and thought this might be a good idea.

I have close to 4 years of experience in software engineering, and felt that having machine learning knowledge would be a good asset to have. I'm purely attempting to learning
machine learning for the sake of building more exciting projects. That in itself is a good enough reason for me. I'm trying not to make it all about ROI. Just pure fun in learning.

## How will this series be structured?

I'll be using the TinyTorch module from MLSys book which teaches us to build TinyTorch from scratch.

Naturally, a bunch of questions will appear in terms of my lack of knowledge in machine learning. I'll be documenting my journey and learning process here.
